Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to digital accessibility.

HotBeans Web is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone and follow relevant accessibility standards.

Our approach

We strive to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 at Level AA wherever practicable. The website is designed with:

  • Semantic HTML and proper heading structure
  • Keyboard-navigable interfaces
  • Sufficient color contrast
  • Descriptive link text and alternative text for images

Measures we take

To support accessibility, we:

  • Use semantic HTML5 and ARIA attributes where needed
  • Label forms clearly for assistive technologies
  • Avoid flashing or flickering content
  • Maintain consistent and predictable navigation

Feedback

We welcome feedback on our website's accessibility. If you encounter barriers or have suggestions, please contact us. We will respond and work to address accessibility issues where reasonably practicable.

Limitations

Some third-party content or linked resources may not fully meet our accessibility standards. We encourage providers of such content to improve accessibility and will seek alternatives when possible.
