Trainee Journeys

Real stories of developers who trained with us and progressed into professional roles within the company. These journeys show how learning, mentorship, and real project experience at HotBeans Web translate into real career opportunities.

Front-End Developer (Trainee)

Marcus Quinn's Journey

Professional Background

Marcus worked in retail management for five years before deciding to change careers. His interest in web development began after helping a friend set up an online store. He started learning HTML and CSS through free resources before enrolling in a structured bootcamp.

Training and Development

Marcus completed a Front End Web Development course covering HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive design, and Git. He also studied accessibility and user experience to strengthen his practical knowledge before applying to HotBeans Web.

Technical Skills

HTML5, CSS3 including Flexbox and Grid, JavaScript ES6+, Git, GitHub, responsive design, browser developer tools, and accessibility standards.

Application Journey

He studied the Job Specifications to understand the role requirements and built a portfolio of responsive projects. He submitted his application through the Application Form with a CV focused on practical skills and project work.

Current Role at HotBeans Web

Marcus works as a Front End Developer Trainee, creating responsive layouts, implementing design specifications, and collaborating with back end developers to integrate APIs.

Advice to Applicants

"Consistency in small projects builds confidence for bigger ones."

Sarah Mitchell's Journey

Professional Background

Sarah graduated with a degree in Business Administration but developed a strong interest in technology and problem solving. After completing an introductory programming course, she chose to focus on back end development through structured online learning.

Training and Development

Sarah completed a Back End Development course covering Node.js, Express, database design, RESTful API development, and authentication. She also studied Linux fundamentals and cloud deployment basics.

Technical Skills

Node.js, Express.js, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, RESTful APIs, authentication methods, Git, environment management, and testing fundamentals.

Application Journey

She matched her skills to the Job Specifications and built a portfolio of API based projects with clear documentation. She applied through the Application Form and was invited for interview based on the quality of her work.

Current Role at HotBeans Web

Sarah works as a Back End Developer Trainee, building server side features, managing databases, and collaborating with front end developers to deliver integrated client solutions.

Advice to Applicants

"Clarity in your code reflects clarity in your thinking."

Adetomiwa Adetoro's Journey

Professional Background

Adetomiwa graduated from Olive Branch College before moving to the United Kingdom to continue his education. At Barnfield College, he completed a Level 3 Computer Science qualification, building foundational knowledge in programming and system design. His early learning came through self directed projects and online tutorials, which helped him understand the basics of development and sparked his interest in pursuing a professional career.

Training and Development

After discovering HotBeans Web, Adetomiwa completed the Full Stack Development course to align his knowledge with industry standards. The course covered front end and back end technologies, version control, and deployment practices. This structured learning helped him understand how development is carried out in real professional environments.

Technical Skills

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6+, React, Node.js, Express, MongoDB, RESTful APIs, Git, GitHub, responsive design, testing fundamentals, and deployment workflows.

Application Journey

He reviewed the Job Specifications carefully and built a portfolio of full stack projects with live demonstrations and detailed documentation. He submitted his application through the Application Form, showing both his technical ability and his learning progression.

Current Role at HotBeans Web

Adetomiwa works as a Full Stack Developer Trainee, contributing to real client projects, participating in code reviews, and building features across the full development stack.

Advice to Applicants

"Progress is proven through what you build, not what you plan."
